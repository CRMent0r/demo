Frequently Asked Questions

Here is the info you need


Q: What types of furniture do you offer?
A: We offer a wide range of furniture for homes, offices, and businesses. Our selection includes sofas, chairs, tables, desks, bookcases, beds, dressers, and more.

Q: What materials do you use for your furniture?
A: We use only the highest quality materials for our furniture, including solid wood, metal, glass, leather, and fabric. We also offer eco-friendly options for customers who are looking for sustainable furniture.
Q: Can I customize my furniture order?
A: Yes, we offer customization options for many of our furniture pieces. You can choose from a variety of finishes, fabrics, and materials to create a piece that is tailored to your specific needs and preferences.
Q: How long does it take to receive my furniture order?
A: The delivery time for your furniture order will depend on the specific item and your location. We will provide you with an estimated delivery date when you place your order.
Q: What is your return policy?
A: We want you to be completely satisfied with your furniture purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return your item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please see our full return policy for more details.
Q: Do you offer financing options?
A: Yes, we offer financing options through select partners. Please contact us for more information on financing options available to you.
